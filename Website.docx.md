**About The Summit**

Theme: **“The Future of GRC: Intelligent, Integrated and Insight-Driven”**

The inaugural **Africa GRC Summit 1.0 (2026)** brings together C-Suite executives, board members, regulators, policymakers and global thought leaders to shape the future of leadership, governance and enterprise resilience across Africa.

In an era defined by rapid digital transformation, cross-border expansion, global integration, regulatory change, geopolitical volatility, disruptive market forces and emerging technologies (GenAI, Agentic AI, and Quantum Computing), this summit responds decisively to these realities by delivering strategic clarity, executive insights and actionable intelligence to navigate these challenges confidently and achieve sustainable growth.

The summit serves as a strategic platform for dialogue, collaboration, insight and actionable initiatives bridging policy, technology and business strategy across Africa's governance, risk, compliance, cybersecurity and technology ecosystem

**Why Africa GRC Summit 1.0**

Africa stands at a pivotal moment. Accelerated digitalization, regulatory evolution, financial inclusion, cross border expansion and technological disruption are redefining how institutions operate and compete. Yet these opportunities also bring heightened risk exposures. Leaders today must confront:

* Escalating cybercrimes and sophisticated digital frauds  
* Financial crimes and operational resilience gaps  
* Increasing regulatory fragmentation and compliance complexities  
* Growing reputational threats driven by misinformation  
* Operational, geopolitical and systemic uncertainties  
* ESG, sustainability and ethical governance pressures  
* Disruptive technologies

The Africa GRC Summit is designed to help leaders anticipate risk, strengthen trust and build resilient institutions in an interconnected global economy
